Why?

1. AI is Destroying the Environment.
AI data-centers in developing areas are destroying communities, ecosystems, and resource supplies in developing nations, creating damages too vast to recount here. Further reading in Empire of AI by Karen Hao if you'd like to hear more on the subject.
2. AI isn't Beneficial to Learning
Copilot AI (Microsoft's rebranding of ChatGPT) will be implemented inside of software like Microsoft Word next year, meaning soon you'll be able to cheat on your homework and never need to open a new tab. It goes without saying, but that isn't amazing for our ability to learn.
3. Microsoft Just Sucks
Can you think of a single month that's gone by without having any problems with Microsoft's products? Glitches aside, this is an American multi-trillion dollar company with a history of major security vulnerabilities in its closed-source software, all of which is a recipe for student data being leaked.
